**From GenAI Day #4 (October 2024):**

- Marc Balcer: Flip TDD -- use AI to extract tests from legacy code, then treat those tests as the spec
- George Churchwell: Generic AI gives generic results; real ROI requires preloading company-specific knowledge
- Om Hashmi: Most AI projects fail not because the tech is bad, but because of poor adoption practices
- Jonathan Stephens: No-code AI democratizes who gets to *build*, not just who gets to *use*
- Abrar Hashmi: Data governance has to come *before* AI deployment -- you can't retrofit ethics

**From GenAI Day #5 (March 2025):**

- Andy Cooper: A leader ran out of tokens mid-session. Andy's verdict: *"You are now a power user."*
- Chris Knotts: *"Amazing how fast the wallets will open"* -- enterprise AI is past the curiosity phase
- Dr. Kenya Oduor: AI as teammate vs. tool -- the difference is whether it adapts to *you* or you adapt to *it*
- Dr. Mark Lane: Agentic AI isn't a future trend -- it's already business-critical
- Julio Fernandez: You can build your own GPT in 5 steps with no technical background
