## Sessions

### Opening Keynote: Leading in the Age of AI Agents -- David Mantica

Explores transformational leadership in an era where "80% of project management tasks will be AI-driven by 2030." Addresses organizational challenges in scaling AI from experimental phases to operational deployment.

#### Notes - Leading in the Age of AI Agents

### Meaning and Purpose in the Age of AI: Making AI Human -- Jordan Plawner

Examines personal and organizational adaptation to AI capabilities. Participants learn approaches to "protect identity, purpose, and creativity" while integrating automation into workflows and team structures.

#### Notes - Meaning and Purpose in the Age of AI: Making AI Human

### Building Custom AI Agents: A Hands-On Guide to Purpose-Built GPTs -- George Churchwell

Practical workshop on designing domain-specific AI applications. Covers prompt engineering techniques and architectural considerations determining whether custom agents become "essential infrastructure or just another tool gathering digital dust."

#### Notes - Building Custom AI Agents: A Hands-On Guide to Purpose-Built GPTs

### AI for Everyone: Practical Tools That Transform How You Work Today -- Tony Rockliff

Demonstrates accessible AI tools for non-technical professionals. Features "live demonstrations of real workflows" covering marketing automation, research assistance, and content generation with immediately applicable techniques.

#### Notes - AI for Everyone: Practical Tools That Transform How You Work Today

### Beyond the Pilot: Bringing AI to Life Across the Organization -- Toby Rao

Strategic framework for organizational AI adoption. Details a case study achieving "58% adoption, a 69% productivity gain, and 93% engagement in nine months" and outlines six key transition strategies.

#### Notes - Beyond the Pilot: Bringing AI to Life Across the Organization

### Scaling AI Agents Without Losing Control: AI Proposes, Systems Decide -- Alan Knox

Addresses governance architecture for autonomous systems. Introduces "governed autonomy" model for managing AI decision-making in regulated, high-stakes environments where risk amplification parallels efficiency gains.

#### Notes - Scaling AI Agents Without Losing Control: AI Proposes, Systems Decide

### The 90-Day Pod: A Framework for Embedding AI Without Losing the Organization -- Stacy Gonyou

Structured methodology for deploying AI teams with defined engagement parameters and sustainable knowledge transfer mechanisms avoiding prolonged organizational dependency.

#### Notes - The 90-Day Pod: A Framework for Embedding AI Without Losing the Organization

### Stop Using a Sledgehammer: Matching AI Autonomy to the Task at Hand -- Jess Wolfe

Introduces the "Five Levels of AI Autonomy" framework for selecting appropriate automation levels. Maps conversational versus task-specific agents to different business scenarios requiring variable levels of machine decision-making authority.

#### Notes - Stop Using a Sledgehammer: Matching AI Autonomy to the Task at Hand

### Stop Automating the Mess: A Lean Case for AI That Actually Works -- Corey King

Advocates applying operational excellence methodologies before AI deployment. Notes "95% of AI initiatives deliver zero return," attributing failures to insufficient diagnostic work before technology selection.

#### Notes - Stop Automating the Mess: A Lean Case for AI That Actually Works

### Elevating SRE Observability using AI -- Dev Panajkar, Prasad Banala

Examines AI applications in site reliability engineering. Covers AI-powered observability reducing noise, enhancing incident investigation, and enabling predictive system reliability practices.

#### Notes - Elevating SRE Observability using AI

### The Moves That Matter: A Practitioner's Guide to Joining the 39% -- Jenny Larsson

Field-based guidance from 20 years across financial services and insurance sectors. Emphasizes "real deployments, real obstacles, real numbers" from heavily regulated industry contexts.

#### Notes - The Moves That Matter: A Practitioner's Guide to Joining the 39%
