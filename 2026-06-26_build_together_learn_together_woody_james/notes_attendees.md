### Essays by Fortune Buchholtz

## Essay 1: High Mood - A Series on Our Augmented Vibe

Buchholtz, F. (2025, May). High mood: A series on our augmented vibe [Substack]. <https://fortunebuchholtz.substack.com/p/high-mood-a-series-on-our-augmented>

**Key Themes:**

- AI and organizational transformation at critical juncture
- Engelbart's augmentation philosophy
- Four levers: artifacts, language, methodology, training
- Bootstrap loops and recursive improvement
- Trustworthiness of AI-generated code
- Personal commitment to self-evolution

**Summary:** We face a pivotal moment where AI, systems thinking, and organizational change converge. Rather than automating broken assumptions, Buchholtz argues Engelbart's 1960s vision of augmentation--enhancing human collaboration and collective capability--remains urgent. The series explores four interconnected levers (artifacts, language, methodology, training) that require co-evolution through bootstrap loops. Examining vibe coding and Kent Beck's "Genie" metaphor, the essay questions how to build trustworthy AI-generated code within intentional socio-technical systems. Real transformation demands people committed to evolving their thinking and capabilities.

## Essay 2: High Mood - A Series on Our Augmented Vibe (Part 2)

Buchholtz, F. (2025, May). High mood: A series on our augmented vibe (Part 2) [Substack]. <https://fortunebuchholtz.substack.com/p/high-mood-a-series-on-our-augmented-33d>

**Key Themes:**

- Augmentation restructures systems, not just accelerates
- H-LAM/T Framework interconnections
- Intelligence as reconfigurable system
- Bootstrapping and recursive co-evolution
- Collective IQ as core capability
- Systemic restructuring over individual gains

**Summary:** Augmentation fundamentally restructures how humans interact with technology, not merely accelerating existing processes. Engelbart's H-LAM/T Framework (Artifacts, Language, Methodology, Training) reveals interconnected levers where changing one requires evolution across all. Intelligence is reconfigurable--capabilities stack hierarchically and evolve through tool engagement. Bootstrapping creates recursive co-evolution where systems improve their own improvement processes, building Collective IQ--the capability to perceive complexity, integrate distributed knowledge, and coordinate action. True augmentation expands collective human potential through systemic restructuring rather than individual performance gains.
