# Goal Definition and Task Breakdown for Generating the OPA Table

## Goal Definition
To identify actionable opportunities from GenAI Day session transcripts, align them with your skills, values, and interests, and create a structured **Opportunity, Practice, Advantage (OPA)** table. This will help you target conversations and potential roles that match your strengths and aspirations.

---

## Task Breakdown

### Step 1: Extract Resume Insights
- **Upload Resume**: You uploaded your resume in DOCX format for analysis.
- **Analyze Resume**: I reviewed your resume and identified key skills, values, and interests.
- **Extract Key Elements**:
  - **Skills**: Training, coaching, technical implementation, Agile facilitation, collaboration, and team dynamics.
  - **Values**: Fostering learning cultures, delivering measurable value, and promoting team alignment.
  - **Interests**: Exploring GenAI, improving team dynamics, and developing innovative solutions.
  - **Additional Context**:
    - Experience with early-stage challenges and undefined metrics.
    - Expertise in fostering collaboration and cross-functional alignment.
    - Proven ability to design training and educational initiatives.
    - Comfort with startup environments and innovation-focused roles.
    - Strong technical fluency in programming, automation, and workflows.

### Step 2: Extract Opportunities
- Review the session transcripts for speakers (e.g., George Churchwell, Samuel Parri, Michael Schank, etc.).
- Identify specific challenges, gaps, or needs mentioned by each speaker.
- Focus on opportunities requiring:
  - Technical, communication, and exploratory skills relevant to your background.
  - Early-stage problem solving and framework creation.
  - Training and team alignment.

### Step 3: Align Opportunities with Your Skills
- Match the identified opportunities with your:
  - **Skills**: Training, coaching, technical implementation, Agile facilitation, and collaboration.
  - **Values**: Fostering learning cultures, delivering measurable value, and promoting team alignment.
  - **Interests**: Exploring GenAI, improving team dynamics, and developing innovative solutions.
  - **Additional Focus**:
    - Opportunities in ambiguous environments requiring metrics or frameworks.
    - Roles prioritizing training, cross-functional collaboration, or coaching.
    - Innovative positions with a startup-like focus.
    - Opportunities with technical fluency requirements.

### Step 4: Populate the OPA Table
- For each speaker and opportunity:
  - **Opportunity**: Describe the gap, challenge, or need identified in the session.
  - **Practice**: Highlight the skills, methods, or tools you bring to address it.
  - **Advantage**: Explain the benefits your involvement could deliver, both for the organization and the broader vision.

### Step 5: Display the Table
- Include columns for:
  - **Number**: Sequential identifier for easy reference.
  - **Speaker**: The speaker associated with the opportunity.
  - **Opportunity**: The identified gap, problem, or area of need.
  - **Practice**: The skills or methods you can bring to address it.
  - **Advantage**: The benefit your involvement could deliver.
  - **Match Score**: Relevance of the opportunity to your skills, values, and interests (1-5 scale).
  - **Impact**: Value the opportunity could bring to customers, with emphasis on high-paying customers (1-5 scale).
  - **Overall**: Product of Match Score and Impact.
