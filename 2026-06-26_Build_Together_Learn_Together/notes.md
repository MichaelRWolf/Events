# Build Together, Learn Together: An AI Workshop

**Date:** Friday, June 26, 2026
**Time:** 9 AM - 11 AM EDT (6-8 AM PDT)
**Format:** Online

## People

- Woody Zuill (organizer)
- James Herr (co-host)

## Announcement

<https://www.eventbrite.com/e/build-together-learn-together-an-ai-workshop-tickets-1992132482883>

This free, interactive workshop invites participants to explore AI-assisted software development
through hands-on collaboration. Teams will work together to build a simple Lean Coffee application
while experimenting with different AI development approaches.

The event emphasizes learning through doing rather than lectures. Participants will discover
practical techniques for effective team collaboration with AI tools, experience rapid
experimentation cycles, and share insights across groups. The workshop welcomes developers,
testers, analysts, managers, and anyone curious about AI -- "no AI expertise required."

Key takeaways include building software with AI assistance, mastering team collaboration
strategies, learning through experimentation, and creating a functional Lean Coffee app. The
experience centers on "what it means to collaborate effectively with AI" and discovering
possibilities when teams and AI work together.
