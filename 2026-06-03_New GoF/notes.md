## Blurb

AI is not just changing how software is implemented -- it is changing the entire development loop.

As implementation becomes increasingly inexpensive and accessible through AI-assisted development, attention shifts upward:

- from coding to intent
- from implementation to validation
- from syntax to design
- from producing software to understanding what software should do and why

In this Roundtable, we will explore how AI is reshaping the way software is defined, built, tested, and evolved.

Topics include:

- defining software through goals, constraints, and context
- AI-assisted development and conversational design
- validation and testing in an era of abundant implementation
- preserving human agency, discernment, and responsibility
- the changing role of developers and architects
- how humans and AI can collaborate effectively without surrendering judgment

We will also explore a deeper question emerging beneath the technical changes:

What becomes most important when implementation is no longer the primary constraint?

This session brings together multiple perspectives on what may be one of the most important discontinuities currently emerging in software development and organizational capability building.
