# Message to Al Shalloway

Al,

I enjoyed seeing you at last week's event and hearing how your thinking has evolved. It certainly helped me feel less of a need to be the 2nd or 5th smartest person in the room.  I'm sure you are having more impact (with coaches and clients) based on this.

Thanks for sharing what you learned and for who you were being.

Best,
Michael
