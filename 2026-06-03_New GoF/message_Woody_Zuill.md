# Message to Woody Zuill

Woody,

I enjoyed seeing you at last week's event.  I know David and Al independently from you, and always appreciate how the transitivity of interests has me bump into people and ideas from different angles.

I keep forgetting your (and Fred's) music background.  I used to invest a lot of time at folk festivals where teaching/learning was integrated with the playing/dancing. Do you think there's a future in that?
